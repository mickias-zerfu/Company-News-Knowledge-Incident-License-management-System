We Help Children Stay Safe, Healthy and Learning.

With over 22 years of experience in the hotel business and a Master's degree in Business Administration specializing in Project Management, Mr. George Constantine is a seasoned professional in hotel operations. His fervent determination and passion for the industry have positioned him as a valuable member of highly successful teams. Since 2019, Mr. Constantine has served as the Chief Secretary under the Metropolitan of Aksum in Ethiopia, where he has also provided support to the Church. In addition to his professional achievements, he is a visionary leader in the world of philanthropy, driven by a steadfast commitment to empowering African communities and promoting sustainable development.

In 2023, Mr. George Constantine founded the CONSTANTINE AFRICAN FOUNDATION, dedicated to driving positive change and transforming lives throughout the continent. Recognizing the challenges faced by African communities, he established the foundation as a catalyst for lasting transformation.

The foundation focuses on key areas such as Algacultures (Food Manufacturing) Education, Healthcare, and Economic empowerment. Understanding the importance of education, Mr. Constantine believes it is the key to unlocking individual and community potential. To that end, the foundation willing established Especially in the field of handicrafts such as carpentry and other crafts, Health Institutes in underserved areas, offered Algacultures scholarships to deserving students, and implemented Health vocational training programs.

Mr. Constantine also believes in the power of economic empowerment. The foundation fosters sustainable economic opportunities through initiatives such as entrepreneurship training, microfinance programs, and support for small-scale farmers and artisans. By uplifting individuals and communities, the foundation contributes to regional development and helps create a more prosperous Africa.

Through his relentless dedication, Mr. George Constantine has made a lasting impact through the CONSTANTINE AFRICAN FOUNDATION. His vision, leadership, and commitment to positive change have inspired countless individuals and organizations to join the cause. Together, they work towards a brighter future for Africa, where access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities is available to all.

Together, they are making a lasting impact and creating a brighter future for Africa.
